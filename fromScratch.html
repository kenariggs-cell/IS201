Ultimate Frisbee

Ultimate Frisbee is a thrilling team sport played with a flying disc, built around speed, strategy, and spirit.

What Makes Ultimate Special?

Ultimate combines athleticism, quick thinking, and a unique culture called the Spirit of the Game. Every player is responsible for fair play and honesty.

Key Skills

  1. Throwing technique
    • Backhand
    • Forehand / Flick
    • Hammer and scoober
  2. Reading the field
    • Cut timing
    • Field positioning
  3. Team communication
    • Calling disc positions
    • Switching defenders

Rules and Roles

Ultimate uses a simple set of rules with no referees in most games. Players call their own fouls and maintain respect for opponents.

  • Seven players per team on the field
    1. Handlers: primary throwers
    2. Cutters: fast movers who attack the end zone
  • No physical contact allowed
  • The disc must be passed within 10 seconds when closely marked

Why Play Ultimate?

From casual pickup games to college tournaments, Ultimate Frisbee is accessible, social, and great for building endurance and teamwork.
