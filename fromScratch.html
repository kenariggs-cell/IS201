What Makes Ultimate Special?

Ultimate combines athleticism, quick thinking, and a unique culture called the Spirit of the Game.

Key Skills

  1. Throwing technique
    • Backhand
    • Forehand / Flick
    • Hammer and scoober
  2. Reading the field
  3. Team communication

Rules and Roles

Players call their own fouls and maintain respect for opponents.

  • Seven players per team
  • No physical contact
  • 10-second stall count
